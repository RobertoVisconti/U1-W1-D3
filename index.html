Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsami vinegear
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead if a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
